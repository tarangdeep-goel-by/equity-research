**Nifty consensus target:** 28,300-29,300 by Dec 2026 (10-14% upside)
**Earnings growth:** 14% FY26, 16% FY27 (Morgan Stanley)

### Structural Tailwinds (Multi-Decade)

1. **Demographics**: 1.04B working-age by 2030, dependency ratio at historic low 31.2%. Consumer spend doubling to $4.3T by 2030.
2. **Formalization**: GST taxpayer base 2.3x (66.5L→1.51Cr since 2017). GST collections doubled in 4 years (18% CAGR). Labour codes enacted Nov 2025.
3. **Digitization**: UPI at 21.6B txns/month, 500M+ users. Digital payments market → $10T by 2026.
4. **China+1**: PLI realized ₹1.76L Cr investment. Electronics exports +137%, semiconductor exports +200%.
5. **Energy Transition**: 500 GW renewable target by 2030. Solar at 133 GW (+41% YoY). $800B private investment planned by 2034.
6. **Defence Indigenization**: ₹7.85L Cr budget (+15% YoY). Domestic procurement at 75%. Exports targeting ₹50,000 Cr by FY29.

### FII/DII Flows

- **DIIs overtook FIIs** for the first time: 17.62% vs 17.22% (March 2025)
- FIIs net sold ₹1.51L Cr in CY2025, but selectively buying back: ₹22,910 Cr into financials, ₹5,661 Cr into telecom
- DII SIP book at ₹25,000+ Cr/month — structural anchor
- FII return expected H2 2026 as rates fall and earnings recover
