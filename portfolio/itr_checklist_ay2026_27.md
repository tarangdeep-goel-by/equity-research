# ITR-2 Filing Checklist — AY 2026-27 (FY 2025-26)

**Due Date: 31 July 2026**
**Tax Regime: New Regime**

---

## Documents to Collect

### From Employer
- [ ] Form 16 (Part A + Part B)

### From Income Tax Portal (incometax.gov.in)
- [ ] Form 26AS
- [ ] Annual Information Statement (AIS) — cross-check all transactions
- [ ] Tax Information Summary (TIS)

### From Groww (Indian Stocks + ETFs + MFs)
- [ ] Capital Gains Statement / Tax P&L Report (FY 2025-26)
- [ ] Dividend income summary
- [ ] Transaction-wise breakdown (for Schedule CG)

### From INDMoney (US Stocks)
- [ ] Capital Gains Statement (FY 2025-26)
- [ ] Dividend income summary (with US tax withheld — needed for DTAA credit)
- [ ] Foreign asset details for Schedule FA (cost of acquisition, date, peak value)
- [ ] Remittance details (INR→USD transfers)

### From HDFC Bank
- [ ] FD Interest Certificate (Form 16A / TDS certificate)
- [ ] PPF Account Statement (FY 2025-26)

### From NPS (CRA / NSDL)
- [ ] NPS Contribution Statement (employer + personal)
- [ ] Check if employer routes your ₹17.5K/month — affects 80CCD(2) deduction

### From Insurer (ULIP)
- [ ] ULIP Premium Payment Statement
- [ ] ULIP Valuation Statement

---

## Key Schedules to Fill

| Schedule | Data Needed |
|---|---|
| **Salary** | Form 16 |
| **Schedule OS** | FD interest, dividends (Indian + US) |
| **Schedule CG** | Capital gains — Groww P&L + INDMoney P&L |
| **Schedule FA** | All US stock holdings (even unsold) — name, country, date acquired, cost, peak value, closing value |
| **Schedule FSI** | US dividends, US capital gains |
| **Schedule TR** | US tax withheld (15%) — to claim DTAA credit |

---

## Notes

- Declare US stocks in Schedule FA even if you didn't sell anything
- LTCG on listed equity/MF: 12.5% above ₹1.25L exemption
- STCG on listed equity/MF: 20%
- Convert US gains to INR using SBI TT buying rate on date of transaction
- Claim US tax credit under India-US DTAA (avoid double taxation)
