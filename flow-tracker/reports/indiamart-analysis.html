The Ownership Story (Jul 2019 → Dec 2025): Post-IPO, Promoter reduced from 52.6% to 49.1% (OFS in early 2021, stable since). FIIs were the early believers — ramping from 5.8% to 27.9% peak (Sep 2021) during the COVID-era tech rally, then steadily exiting to 18.3% as they rotated out of mid-cap internet. MFs were late but decisive — flat at 2-5% until Q4 2023, then aggressive accumulation to 14.6%. Public/retail has steadily shrunk from 34.7% to 16.2% as institutions crowded in. AIFs maintained a small 0.4-1.6% position throughout. The net effect: ownership shifted from retail-heavy to institution-heavy, with the FII→MF handoff being the dominant dynamic of the last 2 years.
