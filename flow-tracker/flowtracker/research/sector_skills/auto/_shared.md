## Auto Sector Caveats

- **Auto is cyclical** — use mid-cycle earnings for valuation, not peak or trough. Current PE may look cheap at cycle peak or expensive at cycle trough
- **EV transition progress**: % of sales from EVs/hybrids is the key structural metric. Companies with credible EV strategies deserve premium valuations
- **Dealer inventory days** (from concall): demand leading indicator. Rising inventory = slowing demand. 20-30 days normal for PVs, 40+ concerning
- **SOTP** for conglomerates (e.g., M&M = auto + farm + financial services, Tata Motors = JLR + India PV + India CV + EV)
- **Raw material basket**: Steel, aluminium, rubber, precious metals. Commodity price cycles affect margins with 1-2 quarter lag

### Annual Report & Investor Deck — Auto Specifics

**AR high-signal sections:**
- `mdna` — volumes by segment (2W/PV/CV/tractors), ASP trend, rural-vs-urban mix, EV transition commentary, export volumes, discounting level.
- `segmental` — PV vs CV vs 2W contribution, domestic vs exports split, spare-parts vs new-vehicle margin.
- `risk_management` — emission-compliance cost, EV capex intensity vs ICE cash-flows, raw-material-basket sensitivity (steel, aluminium, rubber), finance-subsidiary exposure.
- `notes_to_financials` — warranty provisions, product-recall reserves, capex-in-progress on EV platforms.
- `auditor_report` — KAMs on warranty-provision adequacy, impairment tests on legacy ICE manufacturing assets.

**Deck high-signal sub_sections:**
- `charts_described` — segmental volume chart, market-share trajectory, discount-intensity heatmap.
- `strategic_priorities` — EV launch cadence, platform consolidation, component-localisation targets.

**Cross-year narrative cues:** `capital_allocation_shifts` capture ICE-vs-EV capex re-weighting; `biggest_concern` often tracks regulatory (BS-VII, CAFE norms) and input-cost trajectory.
