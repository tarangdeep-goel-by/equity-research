## Pharma Sector Caveats

- **R&D spend is investment, not cost.** High R&D/Revenue (>8%) is a positive signal for pipeline-driven pharma — penalizing margin for R&D intensity misses that this spending creates future revenue streams
- **US price erosion** is structural (~5-8%/year for generics). The key question is whether new launch pipeline offsets erosion. Focus on ANDA filing rate and approval velocity
- **SOTP** is appropriate for complex pharma with multiple business lines (API + formulations + CRAMS/CDMO + biosimilars). Value each segment on relevant multiples
- **Note on sub-sectors:** Hospitals, Diagnostics, and CDMO have fundamentally different frameworks
- Standard PE/DCF valuation works. Growth pharma trades at premium to generics

### Annual Report & Investor Deck — Pharmaceuticals Specifics

**AR high-signal sections (consult proactively when agent is mandated):**
- `mdna` — R&D spend as % of sales, pipeline count, USFDA inspection outcomes (Form 483, warning letters, import alerts), ANDA filings + approvals, biosimilar progression.
- `auditor_report` — KAMs on revenue recognition for deferred-payment drug launches, inventory write-downs, recall provisions; emphasis-of-matter paragraphs on ongoing USFDA matters.
- `risk_management` — regulatory risk (FDA/DCGI), pricing-control exposure (NLEM/DPCO), single-molecule concentration risk.
- `notes_to_financials` — impairment provisions on failed trials, capitalised R&D write-offs, contingent liabilities from product-liability litigation (esp. for US-exposed companies).
- `segmental` — domestic formulations vs US generics vs API vs emerging markets split; margin-by-geo.

**Deck high-signal sub_sections (for Business/Financials/Valuation agents):**
- `strategic_priorities` — pipeline milestones, launch cadence, US generic price-erosion commentary.
- `charts_described` — pipeline progression chart, USFDA plant-status heatmap, ANDA approvals-to-filings ratio.

**Cross-year narrative cues:** Track `biggest_concern` for USFDA escalations year-over-year; `strategic_framing_shift` captures transitions from generics-volume to specialty/biosimilars. `auditor_signals` flag creeping revenue-recognition concerns in specialty-launches.
